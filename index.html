About me

Hello!

I’ve always enjoyed thinking critically and solving problems, thus, having the opportunity to learn and implement web-based language and simultaneously experiencing of my code being actualized in real-life is both interesting and exciting. It is my desire to become an avid supporter and expert of web-based language.
